GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and process.

We specialize in residential renovations, commercial fit-outs, and custom carpentry work. Our team has extensive experience in complete home renovations, office transformations, retail space design, and custom cabinetry and furniture. We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality craftsmanship for projects of all sizes.

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Small carpentry projects may take 2-6 weeks, while full home renovations can take 3-6 months. Commercial fit-outs typically range from 2-4 months. During the consultation phase, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project needs and requirements.

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. We hold all necessary builder's licenses and registrations required in Victoria. Additionally, we maintain comprehensive insurance coverage including public liability insurance and builder's warranty insurance to ensure your project and property are protected throughout the construction process.

We understand that changes may arise during a project. We handle variations through a formal change order process. Any requested changes are documented with detailed cost and time implications, requiring client approval before proceeding. This transparent approach ensures there are no surprises and allows you to make informed decisions about your project.

Yes, all our work comes with a comprehensive warranty. We provide a standard 7-year structural warranty on all building works as required by Victorian law, and a 12-month warranty on all workmanship. Additionally, all fixtures, fittings, and appliances are covered by their respective manufacturer warranties, which we pass on to you with all relevant documentation.

We typically structure payments in milestones aligned with project progress. This usually includes an initial deposit, followed by staged payments at key completion points, and a final payment upon project completion. For larger projects, we provide a detailed payment schedule in our contract. We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, and can discuss other arrangements as needed.

Still have questions? We're here to help.
